Harith albadry

My name is Harith Albadry. I have spent most of my adult life working as a doctor. Most recently as a consultant histopathologist for the NHS. This came after many years working as a junior doctor in various hospital roles. Although medicine was my career IT and design have always been my main passions and I have decided that now is the time to make the leap. I have therefore attained the status of ACE (Adobe certified Associate) in photoshop, illustrator and InDesign. I have also successfully completed a full stack developer bootcamp at the university of Manchester. This is has really helped me develop my IT skills. I found my self good at thinking of alternative ways of solving IT and software problems, a thinking out of the box sort of thing. I really enjoyed brining web designs and UX to life and making a visual impact with my deployments. Medicine has blessed me with many transferable skills including being well organised, excellent time management and being a natural team player with excellent communications. I see this set of skills helping me deliver in my role as a web designer and UX designer in the future / for x company
